47. Storytelling Is Easier Than You Think

Key Frameworks or Concepts: Story Loops, Five Reveal Scenarios, Context Clues.



Specific Advice or Rules: "Start with the interesting reveal points and you work backwards to figure out what context I need to show". "The context part of the loop has to be clear enough so they can get curious in the first place".



Memorable Quotes or Lines: "Include a misdirection every 2 to 3 minutes in the story." (Cross-ref). "Great stories have tons of mini up and downs that live within each loop."
